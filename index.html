Welcome to Pick-a-Flick

Do you have difficulty in picking a movie to watch? You are not alone. It is not an easy task to pick a movie when there are millions of them out there. Bother not, let us help you. Select a genre and click the button to find you a flick from our collection of thousands. All the suggestions we give you have an IMDB rating of more than 5.5.

NB: Please note that this site does not work on IE but works on Edge and all the other modern browsers.
